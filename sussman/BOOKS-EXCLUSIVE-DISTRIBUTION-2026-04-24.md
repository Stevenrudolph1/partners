**Date**: April 24, 2026  
**Effective**: April 24, 2026

## PREAMBLE

This agreement grants Sussman Education exclusive distribution rights for three published books by Multiple Natures International, LLC in the New York City K-12 public school sector. 

Sussman's primary value beyond sales is:
1. **FAMIS Registration** — handling NYC DOE approval and FAMIS system listing for all titles
2. **Curriculum Review Navigation** — managing school district adoption and vetting processes
3. **Market Access** — direct relationships with NYC school administrators and procurement

In exchange, Sussman receives:
1. Exclusive geographic and channel rights in NYC K-12 schools (3 years)
2. 40% wholesale discount on all titles
3. Performance-based renewal with automatic escalation if revenue targets are met
4. Right of first refusal on new MN-authored books and derivatives

**Expansion Rights**:
- Sussman may expand to **adjacent territories** (New Jersey, Connecticut, Greater Boston) with **30-day written notice** to MNI
- Expansion territories maintain exclusive status (Sussman is sole distributor)
- If expanding beyond adjacent territories (e.g., national distribution), Sussman must renegotiate terms or lose NYC exclusivity

## 2. PRICING & DISCOUNT

### 2.1 Wholesale Discount

MNI offers Sussman a **40% wholesale discount** off the list price of all Licensed Books.

**Example**:
- Licensed Book retail price: $25.00
- Wholesale price to Sussman: $15.00 (60% of retail)
- Sussman may set any retail price they choose

### 2.2 Print-on-Demand Ordering

MNI will print Licensed Books via **Lulu.com** (print-on-demand platform).

**Ordering process**:
1. Sussman orders directly from Lulu as a reseller (Sussman must register for Lulu reseller account)
2. Lulu applies the 40% wholesale discount to orders
3. MNI will coordinate reseller account setup with Lulu if needed
4. **No minimum order quantities** — Sussman may order 1 or 100 units per title
5. **Lead times**: Lulu standard lead time is 1–2 weeks; Sussman should order 3–4 weeks before anticipated school adoption/usage

### 2.3 Inventory & Returns

- **Inventory risk**: Sussman holds all inventory risk (standard for resellers)
- **Returns**: Lulu and MNI do not accept returns on print-on-demand orders (standing policy)
- **Defects**: If Sussman receives damaged/defective copies from Lulu, Sussman should work directly with Lulu for replacement (covered under Lulu's quality guarantee, not MNI)

### 2.4 Payment Terms

- Sussman pays Lulu directly for each order
- No invoicing between MNI and Sussman for book sales (direct POD model)
- **Reporting obligation**: Sussman must provide MNI with quarterly sales reports showing units sold by title (see Section 3.2)

## 3. MINIMUM SALES COMMITMENTS & PERFORMANCE

### 3.1 Tiered Annual Minimum Commitments

Sussman must achieve the following **annual net revenue** commitments (revenue from actual book sales, not inventory purchases):

**Year 1 (April 2026 – March 2027)**: 
- **$30,000** annual minimum
- Quarterly targets: $7,500 per quarter

**Year 2 (April 2027 – March 2028)**:
- **$50,000** annual minimum
- Quarterly targets: $12,500 per quarter

**Year 3 (April 2028 – March 2029)**:
- **$75,000** annual minimum
- Quarterly targets: $18,750 per quarter

### 3.2 Sales Reporting & Verification

Sussman must provide MNI with **quarterly sales reports** within **15 days** of each quarter end:

- Units sold by title
- Net revenue by title
- Cumulative revenue (year-to-date)
- Marketing activities / school adoption status
- Forecast for next quarter

**Reporting deadline**: 15 days after end of each quarter (July 15, October 15, January 15, April 15)

### 3.3 Performance Triggers & Cure Rights

**If Sussman misses quarterly minimum**:

1. **First miss**: MNI sends written notice; Sussman has **30 days** to explain shortfall or commit to catch-up plan
2. **Second consecutive miss**: MNI may issue notice of exclusivity suspension pending cure
3. **Third consecutive miss** (two quarters below target in same contract year): MNI may terminate exclusivity and convert to non-exclusive distribution (Section 5.2)

**Cure mechanisms**:
- Sussman may propose alternative targets (e.g., schools are moving slower than expected) with supporting documentation
- If approved by MNI, adjusted targets apply for remainder of that quarter/year
- No more than **one adjustment per contract year**

### 3.4 Year-End True-Up

If annual revenue falls **$5,000 or more** below the target, MNI may:
- Require a written action plan for Year 2 to reach targets
- Reduce discount by 2–5% (if performance deteriorates further)
- Exercise termination right (Section 5.2) if trend continues into Year 2

## 4. FAMIS REGISTRATION & SCHOOL APPROVAL

### 4.1 Sussman's FAMIS Responsibility

Sussman agrees to take primary responsibility for:

1. **FAMIS System Registration**: Registering all Licensed Books in the NYC DOE FAMIS purchasing system
2. **Vendor Status**: Maintaining Sussman's status as an approved vendor in FAMIS
3. **Curriculum Review Support**: Supporting school district curriculum committees with information about the Licensed Books for adoption review
4. **Documentation**: Providing schools with:
   - Curriculum alignment documents
   - Sample chapters or excerpts
   - Teacher/counselor implementation guides
   - Pricing and bulk order information

### 4.2 MNI's Support

MNI will provide Sussman with:
- Ready-to-share curriculum materials
- Author availability for occasional school presentations or professional development (2–3 events per year, MNI covering travel costs if within NYC/NJ area)
- Marketing materials for school adoption campaigns
- Any third-party reviews or endorsements

### 4.3 School Approval Timeline

Schools typically require **60–120 days** for curriculum review and adoption approval. This is **not MNI's gate**; it is the school district's process.

Sussman must:
- Initiate school outreach and curriculum review requests
- Track adoption timeline with schools
- Report progress to MNI quarterly (Section 3.2)

## 5. TERM, RENEWAL & TERMINATION

### 5.1 Initial Term

**Duration**: 3 years from execution of this agreement (April 24, 2026 – April 24, 2029)

### 5.2 Automatic Renewal

This agreement automatically renews for successive **1-year periods** unless either party provides **90-day written notice** of non-renewal.

**Exception**: If exclusivity has been suspended or terminated under Section 3.3, renewal is **not automatic** and must be renegotiated.

### 5.3 Performance-Based Renewal Escalation

**If Sussman achieves or exceeds all annual targets in Years 1–2**:
- Term automatically extends to **5 years** (April 24, 2029 – April 24, 2031)
- Discount remains 40%
- Minimum commitments increase by 5% annually (Year 3 becomes $79K, Year 4 $83K, etc.)

**If Sussman falls short in Year 2**:
- Renewal is month-to-month at MNI's option
- Discount may be reduced to 35% for year 3 renewal
- Minimum commitments may be reduced or renegotiated

### 5.4 Termination for Cause

Either party may terminate this agreement immediately if:

1. **Material breach**: The other party materially breaches this agreement and fails to cure within **30 days** of written notice
2. **Approved vendor status loss**: If Sussman loses approved vendor status with NYC DOE FAMIS without reasonable cause, MNI may terminate (Note: MNI will work with Sussman to restore status before exercising termination)
3. **Insolvency**: If either party becomes insolvent or files for bankruptcy

### 5.5 Termination for Convenience (Non-Exclusive Only)

If exclusivity has been suspended or converted to non-exclusive (Section 3.3), either party may terminate with **60-day written notice**.

### 5.6 Wind-Down Period

Upon termination or non-renewal:

- **60-day wind-down period** begins
- Sussman may complete existing school orders and sales through the wind-down period
- Sussman loses all rights to use MNI trademarks, materials, and brand assets after wind-down period
- Sussman must return or destroy all branded marketing materials
- MNI may license the titles to other distributors effective immediately upon termination

- Use the MN logo or trademark for non-book products without written approval
- Claim authorship or co-authorship of the Licensed Books
- Create marketing materials that misrepresent the content or curriculum alignment

### 6.3 Quality Assurance

MNI retains the right to review Sussman's marketing materials and request revisions if they:
- Misrepresent the books' content
- Violate MNI's branding guidelines
- Include false curriculum alignment claims

MNI will provide written feedback within **10 business days** of receiving materials for review.
