## Current Negotiation

### The Ask
MNI asks Sussman to fund the $25K upgrade + audit.
MNI reimburses from first sales.

### Sussman's Question
"We're laying out $25K+ — what upside do we get for taking this risk?"

### Proposed Options (Ranked)

#### Option 1 — Temporary Enhanced Split (Recommended)
- Sussman funds $25K
- Reimbursed from first sales
- After reimbursement: 60/40 in Sussman's favor until they earn additional $40-50K premium
- Then revert to 50/50
- **Clean cap. No permanent distortion.**

#### Option 2 — Volume-Based Escalator
- 0–$125K → 50/50
- $125K–$250K → 55/45 Sussman
- $250K+ → 60/40 Sussman
- Must preserve $20 minimum/test to MNI

#### Option 3 — Exclusivity Reset + Term Extension
- New 3-year term begins upon FAMIS approval
- Minimums reset from activation date
- 50/50 maintained (enhanced split Year 1 only)
- Must include activation deadline trigger

#### Option 4 — Right of First Refusal on AI MNTEST
- NOT automatic exclusivity
- Just right of first refusal for AI version in NYC once compliant
- Signals long-term partnership without giving away control

#### Option 5 — Fixed Risk Premium
- Reimbursement from first sales
- Plus $10-15K fixed premium upon hitting revenue threshold
- Simpler, more predictable
